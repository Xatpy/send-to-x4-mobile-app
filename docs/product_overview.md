# Product Overview: Send to X4 (Mobile)

## 🚀 Product Vision
**"The bridge between the boundless web and your focused Xteink X4."**

Send to X4 transforms the chaotic experience of reading on a smartphone into the serene, focused experience of reading on e-ink. It is designed **exclusively for the Xteink X4**, empowering users to capture content from anywhere—browsers, social media, news apps—and instantly transfer it to their device in a clean, book-like format.

---

## 📋 Product Brief
**Name:** Send to X4
**Platform:** iOS / Android (React Native)
**Device Compatibility:** **Xteink X4 Only**
**Status:** Beta / Active Development

**The Problem:** Reading long-form content or complex threads on a backlit phone screen is straining and distracting. Transferring files to niche e-readers usually involves cables, computers, and manual file conversion.

**The Solution:** An elegant mobile utility tailored for the **Xteink X4** that accepts shared links, scrapes the content (even from complex sites like X/Twitter), generates a polished EPUB, and pushes it wirelessly to the device via a direct WiFi connection.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Seamless "Share to X4"
Integrated directly into the OS share sheet. Users never have to manually copy-paste URLs (though they can). From Chrome, Safari, or the X app, it's just **Share > Send to X4**.

### 2. Advanced Content Extraction
- **Reader Mode**: Strips ads, popups, and clutter from standard processing.
- **Specialized Twitter/X Engine**: Uses a custom, headless browser approach to fully render and capture long text posts, threads, and media that standard scrapers miss.

### 3. Direct WiFi Transfer
- **No Cloud Required**: Files go straight from phone to device.
- **Privacy First**: Your reading habits aren't tracked on a server.
- **Universal Support**: Compatible with both **Stock** firmware and **CrossPoint** open-source firmware.

### 4. Integrated File Manager
- View the files currently on your X4 device.
- Delete old articles directly from the app.
- Auto-refresh ensures your view is always in sync with the device.

## 🎤 How to Present the App (The Pitch)

**To a Casual User:**
> "Stop squinting at your phone. If you find a long article or a great thread, just 'Send to X4'. It’ll be ready on your e-reader in seconds, formatted like a real book."

**To a Power User / Techie:**
> "This is a specialized tool for your X4. It handles the heavy lifting of HTML scraping and EPUB generation locally. It even spins up a headless view to properly capture client-side rendered apps like Twitter. It speaks the native API of both Stock and CrossPoint firmwares, giving you full control over your offline library without touching a USB cable."

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
- **Dark Mode Native**: Built with a sleek, dark aesthetic to match the premium hardware feeling.
- **Subtle Interactions**: Uses animations (pulsing inputs, smooth transitions) to signal state changes without being noisy.
- **Feedback-Rich**: Clear status indicators for connection, extraction, and transfer stages so the user never guesses what's happening.
